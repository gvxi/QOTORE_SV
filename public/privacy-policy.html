1. Introduction

Welcome to Qotore, your premier destination for luxury fragrances in Oman. We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, place orders, or interact with our services.

Qotore operates as an online fragrance retailer, offering a curated selection of premium fragrances in various sizes including 5ml, 10ml, 30ml samples, and full bottles. We are based in Muscat, Oman, and primarily serve customers throughout the Sultanate of Oman.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you place an order or interact with our services, we collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Contact Information: Full name, phone number, email address
  • Delivery Information: Complete delivery address, city, region/wilayat, special delivery instructions
  • Order Information: Fragrance preferences, quantities, order notes and special requests
  • Communication Records: Messages sent through WhatsApp, email, or other communication channels

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type and version, operating system
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, time spent on site, click patterns, referring websites
  • Local Storage: Shopping cart contents, preferences, and session information stored locally on your device
  • Performance Data: Website loading times, error reports, and system diagnostics

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from third-party services we use:

  • Payment Processors: Transaction confirmations and payment status (when applicable)
  • Delivery Services: Delivery confirmations and status updates
  • Analytics Services: Aggregated website usage statistics

5. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement comprehensive measures to protect it:

5.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All data transmission is protected using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Hosting: Our website and databases are hosted on secure, professionally managed servers
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and updates are applied promptly
  • Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for security threats and vulnerabilities

5.2 Administrative Safeguards

  • Regular security training for all staff members
  • Confidentiality agreements for anyone with access to customer data
  • Regular review and updating of security policies and procedures
  • Incident response procedures for any security breaches

5.3 Physical Safeguards

  • Secure facilities with controlled access
  • Protected storage of any physical records
  • Secure disposal of sensitive information when no longer needed

‚ö†Ô∏è Security Reminder

While we implement strong security measures, please remember to keep your personal information safe. Do not share your order details or personal information with unauthorized parties claiming to represent Qotore.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected:

6.1 Active Customer Data

  • Order Information: Retained for 3 years after order completion for customer service and warranty purposes
  • Account Information: Retained while you remain an active customer and for 2 years after last activity
  • Communication Records: Retained for 1 year for customer service quality and training purposes

6.2 Legal and Business Requirements

  • Tax Records: Retained for 7 years as required by Omani tax law
  • Fraud Prevention: Certain data may be retained longer for fraud prevention and security purposes
  • Legal Disputes: Information may be retained longer if involved in legal proceedings

6.3 Data Deletion

When personal information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it. You may also request deletion of your personal information subject to legal and business requirements.

10.1 Age Restrictions

14. Glossary of Terms

To help you understand this Privacy Policy, here are definitions of key terms:

14.1 Data Protection Terms

  • Personal Information: Any information that can identify you as an individual
  • Processing: Any operation performed on personal data, including collection, storage, and use
  • Data Controller: The entity (Qotore) that determines how and why personal data is processed
  • Data Processor: Third parties who process personal data on our behalf
  • Consent: Your freely given agreement to our processing of your personal information

14.2 Technical Terms

  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to remember preferences and improve experience
  • Local Storage: Data stored in your browser for website functionality
  • Encryption: Technology that protects data by converting it into a secure code
  • SSL/TLS: Security protocols that protect data transmitted over the internet
  • IP Address: A unique number assigned to your device when connected to the internet

14.3 Business Terms

  • Order Processing: All activities involved in handling your fragrance orders
  • Customer Service: Support provided to help with orders, questions, and concerns
  • Marketing Communications: Promotional messages about products and offers
  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us operate our business
